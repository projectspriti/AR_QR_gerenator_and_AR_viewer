How to use:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Point your device at a flat surface (table, floor, etc.)
  3. Wait for the surface to be detected (you'll see tracking indicators)
  4. Tap on the detected surface to place the 3D model
  5. Move around to view the model from different angles
