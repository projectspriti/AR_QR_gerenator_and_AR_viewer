How to use:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Point your device at a flat surface (table, floor, etc.)
  3. Move the camera slowly until a surface is detected (you'll see "Surface Detected" message)
  4. When the camera stops on the detected surface, tap anywhere to place the 3D model
  5. The model will appear anchored to the surface - move around to view from different angles
